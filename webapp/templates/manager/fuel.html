Estimation Methodology

Fuel is estimated from dwell_time_seconds on EXIT gate events using a litres-per-operational-hour factor per vehicle type:

Motorcycle: 3 L/hr Car: 8 L/hr Van: 10 L/hr Utility: 12 L/hr Truck: 20 L/hr

Values are estimates for cross-vehicle comparison, not metered readings. Actual consumption depends on load, route, and driver behaviour.
