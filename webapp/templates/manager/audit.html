How the Audit Chain Works

Every row inserted into access_log is cryptographically linked to the previous row via SHA-256:

hash_n = SHA256(JSON({ plate, timestamp, gate_id, direction, hash_(n-1) }))

Row 1 uses the genesis salt VAAS-GENESIS-2026 as its seed. Any modification of a row — plate number, timestamp, gate, or direction — breaks the chain at that point and all rows beyond it.
