Anomaly Definitions

Events written to access_log when a direction inconsistency is detected for a registered, active vehicle. The barrier opens in both cases — vehicles are never denied entry due to a logging gap.

DOUBLE ENTRY Vehicle presented at entry while its last event was also an entry. Likely cause: prior exit not captured (obscured plate, tailgate, wrong lane).
UNMATCHED EXIT Vehicle at exit gate with no matching entry on record. Likely cause: entry missed, or vehicle entered before VAAS was operational for that shift.
